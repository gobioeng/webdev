Fundamentals of Biomedical Engineering

Author: John Doe

Edition: 3rd Edition (2024)

Publisher: Academic Press

ISBN: 978-1234567890

Description: A comprehensive guide to the principles and applications of biomedical engineering. This textbook covers everything from basic concepts to advanced topics, including biomaterials, biomechanics, medical imaging, biosensors, and tissue engineering. Suitable for undergraduate and graduate students in biomedical engineering programs.

Buy on Amazon

Neural Engineering Handbook

Author: Jane Smith

Edition: 2nd Edition (2023)

Publisher: Technical Press

ISBN: 978-0987654321

Description: This handbook provides an in-depth look at neural engineering, covering topics such as brain-computer interfaces, neural prosthetics, neuromodulation, and computational neuroscience. It's an essential resource for researchers, engineers, and clinicians working in the field of neural engineering and neuroprosthetics.

Buy on Amazon

Medical Imaging Systems

Author: Robert Johnson

Edition: 4th Edition (2022)

Publisher: Medical Press

ISBN: 978-5678901234

Description: A comprehensive overview of modern medical imaging technologies and their clinical applications, including X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound, and nuclear medicine imaging systems. The book covers both the physical principles and the engineering aspects of medical imaging, making it suitable for biomedical engineers, medical physicists, and radiologists.
